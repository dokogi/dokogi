SURA NYA FATIHA.

MAKACI, AYAUZHI (7), EGANMINSUNZHI (000), HARAFIUZHI (0000).

Translated By Sheikh Muhammad Pati Emisheshi R.T.A

ﭑﭒﭓﭔﭕ

1. Miaca be sunna sokoe, tsoci na’a niimauzhi keyiba , muminizhi to kafirizhi emi yizheona, tsoci na-ada yegun’ukpe muminizhiri ke kafirizhi lugwa efo kiyamae na.

ﭖﭗﭘﭙﭚ

2. Yebojin nya Alitazhi kpata wun’ajin tabbata ya SOKO, Tsoci naekpa Alitazhi kpaata na.

ﭛﭜﭝ

3. Tsoci na-a ni’imau-zhi keyiba muminizhi to kafirizhi Emi yizheo na, Tsoci na-ada yegun’ukpe muminizhiri ke kafirizhi lugwa Efo kiyamae na.

ﭞﭟﭠﭡ

4. Tsoci na’ajin sheriya efo egagbaena.

ﭢﭣﭤﭥﭦ

5. Woe (soko) ga yiyiejin bawta yao, Woe (soko) ga yiyiewa taimakozhi beyin’o.

ﭧﭨﭩﭪ

6. Wuyiba nya dida eko gboro (ugayi Adini yan gaskiyao).

ﭫﭬﭭﭮﭯﭰﭱﭲ ﭳﭴ

7. Eko nya zanazhi wotwaria nya niimazhi ya na, bambe eko nya zanazhi woyiayin nyangban beyin na, mimagan zanazhiagbin na-a.
