QURAN TRANSLATION BY SHEIKH MUHAMMAD PATI EMISHESHI R.T.A ALHAMDULILLAH

SURATUL HAKATU.

MAKACI, AYAUZHI (52), EGANMINSUNZHI (256), HARAFIUZHI (1,480).

SURATUL HAKATU, MAKA BO ACHIUN YA ANNABIO, AYAUZHI UYI ARATA (50) EGAN MISUUN ZHI, KPAKO CHIATU ARATA BE GUTSWAN`YIN`I (256), HARAFIU ZHI KPUUTSUN CHIATU SHITSUN EDA GUNI`I BE SHINI`I (1480).

354

Mia cha be sunnan SOKOe, etsu naa niimau keyiba to mumini zhi to kafiri zhi emi izheo nan, etsu naada yeguun kpe mumini zhi ri efo kiyamae ke kafiri zhi lugwa nan.

ﮯﮰ

1. Efo elatu yan gaskiya.

ﮱﯓﯔ

2. kye`ye efo elatu gaskiyao.

ﯕﯖﯗﯘﯙ

3. Kimaayawe kpikpe yan akika efo gaskiyao.

ﯚﯛﯜﯝﯞ

4. Samudawa chizhi (jemaa yan annabi swalihu zhi) to Adawa chizhi (jemaa yan annabi hudu zhi) a efo nan yangban alita zhia che`e kun be kagboe nan yi gan-ma-nan.

ﯟﯠﯡﯢﯣ

5. Samudawa chi zhi (jemaa yan annabi swalihu zhi) achia jin halaka be egun-kpoe nan de tsan-nani be kagboe nan.

ﯤﯥﯦﯧﯨﯩﯪ

6. Adawa chi zhi (jemaa yan annabi hudu zhi) achia jin halaka be dundufe yan azaba nan fuyeko be kagboe umaeti be kagboe nan.

ﯫﯬﯭﯮﯯﯰﯱﯲﯳﯴﯵﯶﯷﯸﯹﯺ

7. Tsochia dundufe wun chin jin foro ta etia bo hari yeshi gutwaba be yigidi gutotae nan`a mitu dozhi jereyin nan, wale jemaa zhiye minin`ubo anikin zhi zhi, ke wayegan chigban tun yan donbinan ayio nan`a gbin ya chi chi unchi de anfanin ndondoa nan.

ﯻﯼﯽﯾﯿﰀ

8. Kashi wole zaye kie minia bo najin halaka`a nan.

ﭑﭒﭓﭔﭕﭖﭗ

9. Fir`auna wunchi be to jemaa nan zhi shiu dzo nan, sojiu zhi nan zhi shiu dzo achi da-gbin nun wan nan, to jemaa ndochi zhi nan zhi akpefiy minin yan kinbo nan, aga yi jemaa yan annabi luutu zhi nan zhi le izhie be leifi SOKOE nan.

ﭘﭙﭚﭛﭜﭝﭞ

10. Kafiri zhi chia le foro yan eyanwu yan tuchi tsochia ba, achia wan win wan natsoyi kpantsuku nan (wato a`a jin azaba nan de tsan nani nan).

ﭟﭠﭡﭢﭣﭤﭥﭦ

11. Akika lokachi nan yi (SOKO) zan nunwan tufanu zaman yan annabi nuhui nan, yi (SOKOA) ye kufyi eshin kukuru yan yanman chi ye zhio fyi minin eya mujeza yan annabi nuhuo.

ﭧﭨﭩﭪﭫﭬﭭ

12. Ebo yi (SOKO`i) la etan wun chin`u fe sisan tu`u yaye uma`u laufe egan nan gaaye tukpa nae wogan unchye layewo yiu nan.

ﭮﭯﭰﭱﭲﭳﭴ

13. Abekie aga efe befi kafo nan efebe yan zun nin nan.

ﭵﭶﭷﭸﭹﭺﭻ

14. fowunchin achigaa kin nanan to patizhi laazhe dozhi be kagboe, lila zhe dozhi yan zunin kyete.

ﭼﭽﭾﭿ

15. Fowun chin yan nan gaajin faruwa nan gaajin.

ﮀﮁﮂﮃﮄﮅ

16. Sanman gaabanfi fowun chin unchi gaagban ke yan nan dan bayipao nan, unchi gaa to gbo gun.

ﮆﮇﮈﮉﮊﮋﮌﮍﮎﮏﮐ

17. To malaika zhi chi gaa dzun tin ya kpe sanmao kapa ndondo, malaika ndochi zhi za gutota aga gado Alarashi yan tso chi woe la dan fitiabo fowunchin`i.

ﮑﮒﮓﮔﮕﮖﮗ

18. Fowunchin aga alitazhi kpata kuche zhi fi`i lio bayachi naan kafo yan zubachi tso be nan, zan dondo ma`a so SOKO minin ye bua.

ﮘﮙﮚﮛﮜﮝﮞﮟﮠﮡ

19. Zaeni ndondo nan aga takarda yan etun`u layau be egwa gwa lwou yin nan, wun`a chie gan gan ego ye gba takarda yan min eti yang-ban fao.

ﮢﮣﮤﮥﮦﮧ

20. Akika mia gaskiya ya kede mia gun be egagba yan yangban fae.

ﮨﮩﮪﮫﮬ

21. Mumini chi ga fed-an rayiwa ndochio nan SOKOo tswa yerda jin be yin nan.

ﮭﮮﮯﮰ

22. Dan mini Alijannao na`a tula nan.

ﮱﯓﯔ

23. Yangichi sun sun`u zhi ga`a tsoba ezami Alijenna zhi kpan yin.

ﯕﯖﯗﯘﯙﯚﯛﯜﯝ

24. Echie gi (yangichi manangi zhi) ye mae chie fin nunwan (managi zhi) ni`iman yan SOKO ayi ya ye yo, ebo etun guru nan zhi`i yelashidzo dan yegboro yebo minin efo izhie yan ye nan zhi yele gogan lenan.

ﯞﯟﯠﯡﯢﯣﯤﯥﯦﯧﯨ

25. Am`ma`a zanan aga la takarda yan etun`u yiu be egwa gwa pin`u yin nan, wun tsoa chie gan gan ka`a sa re, ala litafi min ya min yan`a. (wunchin mayi ebo nan alamariu wun`a zofi egba be zunye sun`i nan).

ﯩﯪﯫﯬﯭ

26. Mi ma kpe iri etan yan egaaba azaba nan SOKOA laya mina`a (eba ma`a nao wato mi de kpi kpe yan kendoe birkichie yan min`a yiyi na`a).

ﯮﯯﯰﯱ

27. Wun` chia gan gan, O`o mi`a-ya, Da achi gan azaba naga wun jin halaka, rayi win chiga`a lakeya bo nan yio wun`a mafi wun gan, natso rayi`a dan nakan wunbo azaba chia chie gi wun nan.

ﯲﯳﯴﯵﯶﯷ

28. (Uchia gangan) ewo nan mide kuyekan izhio nan unkunke yamin woa be azaba yan SOKO yin`a.

ﯸﯹﯺﯻ

29. Uchia gangan huja yan mi (eti etsu ko ewo nan dan be mi yin nan) wun`a fe halaka ya min.

ﯼﯽﯾ

30. SOKO chia gan malaika zhi gan ewau`n yela wun`e pa.

ﯿﰀﰁﰂ

31. Kanga ganchin enan yan jehimi bo yea law gun`o.

ﰃﰄﰅﰆﰇﰈﰉﰊ

32. Kanga ganchin minin yan dzari yan azaba nan ekpawun`u yi gaba adwani nan, yalagun`u misun`u bo yeche`e dae wa wun`e tin`e ya misun`u bo`o.

ﰋﰌﰍﰎﰏﰐﰑ

33. Akika kafiri wun`a fe zana ya gaskiya be SOKO etsu nan chinwan wun`a zankpe nan yin`a nan.

ﰒﰓﰔﰕﰖﰗ

34. Umae din rayi zawangi zhi zhin`a ebo achia ya ezagwa gbagi zhi yan gichi`a gi`a.

ﭑﭒﭓﭔﭕﭖ

35. Ebo achin`o yinan eya machi ndoadoa danbo nan`a ma byu yin na`a.

ﭗﭘﭙﭚﭛﭜ

36. Yangi chi ndochi ma dabo`a ya egi minan zhi`a sai dai (gisli`i ni) ugayi egya be ebi nan` ga`e tinya nakan kpache yan egi minan zhio nan.

ﭝﭞﭟﭠﭡ

37. Zeinin dondo ma`a gi wun`a sei dei leifi jinchi zhi.

ﭢﭣﭤﭥﭦ

38. (SOKO gan) Akika min`e zhiba be yan nan zhi yeye leye nan (ugayi sanman to kin to yigidi to etswa to tswangi zhi be yandochi nazhi tula nan).

ﭧﭨﭩﭪ

39. Mi mae zhiba be yan nan zhi ye ye`e leyea nan (ugayi alijennan to enan to kpandara siran) be eyan susochi ndochi zhi`i, na eyea leye izhe boa nan.

ﭫﭬﭭﭮﭯ

40. Akika AL QUR`ANU egan misun uyio yan tuchi eza chinwan chi.

ﭰﭱﭲﭳﭴﭵﭶﭷﭸ

41. QUR`AN, ujin egan yan enikochi zhyi`a, amma`a ezadegi zhi ga`a ya gaskiyao.

ﭹﭺﭻﭼﭽﭾﭿﮀ

42. QUR`ANU man ujin egan yan eba sanchi zhyi`a, amma`a eza degizhi egae gwo sisan two`o.

ﮁﮂﮃﮄﮅ

43. QUR`ANU achiun chin zhin be ya Annabi (S.A.W) dag-a eba etsu nan`e kpa alitazhi kpata nan.

ﮆﮇﮈﮉﮊﮋ

44. Da achi gan Annabi MUHAMMADU (S.A.W) gan gan ndochi gan yan eti tachiun nan tso jin yan mi yin`a nan.

ﮌﮍﮎﮏ

45. Da yi gauwan kpantsukwi`i gwa`alo`o.

ﮐﮑﮒﮓﮔ

46. Gan chin yi chi ga biba ladan kagbo ndondo daga ebawboo.

ﮕﮖﮗﮘﮙﮚﮛ

47. Akika a`a de zeinin dondo miniyebo nan`a kan gwa yan azaba yiu wo nan`a.

ﮜﮝﮞﮟ

48. Akika QUR`AN sisantu uyio ya zanan zhye sun dan SOKO nan.

ﮠﮡﮢﮣﮤﮥ

49. Akika yi SOKO de kpikpe kede akika ade minin yebo nan zhi yi gan manan zunchi ayo`o nan.

ﮦﮧﮨﮩﮪ

50. Akika ma` QUR`ANU wun`a da`a zhe ngakpe ya kafiri zhi.

ﮫﮬﮭﮮ

51. Akika QUR`ANU gaskiya yan eye be ye uyio.

ﮯﮰﮱﯓﯔ

52. Ebo achion ANNABI (S.A.W) chye`e li` tsochi woe etsu nan`a gbota chin wan`o nan.
